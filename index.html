WHO ARE WE

Located at
Plot 1 Block VII, Oba Afunbiowo Housing Estate Akure, Ondo State. St Simeon's is a multi-cultural community of people of all ages, celebrating christianity through christian education, outreach and worship.

Whatever your background, you will find a warm welcome with us.

Our weekly activities includes:

  1. Sunday morning worship is held by 8:30am at the church premises.
  2. We also have an evening house fellowship every Sunday evening at Dr Olowosusi's residence by 5:30pm.
  3. We also hold mid-week prayer meeting every wednesday by 5:30pm at the church premises.

Meet Our Vicar

Rev Abiodun O Omotosho

St Simeon's Anglican Church,Idanre, Akure, Ondo State, Nigeria, was established on 10th of January 2016. The Church is pragmatically committed to the development of her members in all ramifications.The Church is committed to the wholistic teaching of our Lord Jesus Christ. Through these wholistic ministry, her members is been developed both physically and spiritually. The activities of the church includes: Sunday service by 8:30am, which is an English service The weekly activities of the church are: Prayer meetings, bible study, Evangelism/Visitation and the Church Choir rehearsal.
