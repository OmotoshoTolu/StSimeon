The History Of St Simeon's Anglican Church (2016 - Present)

To the glory of God, a maiden sunday service was held on the 10th of January 2016 at Dr Olowosusi residence and the service was conducted by Rev. Can L.K Komolafe. The Diocesan Rt Rev'd Dr S.O Borokini Ph.D layed the foundation of the Sanctuary on 18th of November, 2016.

The first year anniversary was celebrated on the 8th of January, 2017 with Holy Communion service celebrated by Rev Akinola. To the glory of God, the proposed children church was roofed on the 28th of June, 2018. We had our maiden sunday worship service inside the new church building, on the 1st of July, 2018.

On the 15th of July, 2019 The Diocesan Rt Rev'd Dr S.O Borokini Ph.D and Princess Borokini in company of other priests paid an episcopal visit to the church, for the naming ceremony programme with the name "SAINT SIMEON'S" to the glory of God.

The first baptismal ceremony service took place on the 6th of October, 2019 those baptised were Sis Lillian Olatunbosun and Bro Oluwapelumi Omotosho.
