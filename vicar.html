Rev Abiodun O Omotosho

St Simeon's Anglican Church,Idanre, Akure, Ondo State, Nigeria, was established on 10th of January 2016. The Church is pragmatically committed to the development of her members in all ramifications.

The Church is committed to the wholistic teaching of our Lord Jesus Christ. Through these wholistic ministry, her members is been developed both physically and spiritually. The activities of the church includes: Sunday service by 8:30am, which is an English service.

The weekly activities of the church are:

  • Prayer meetings,
  • Bible study,
  • Evangelism/Visitation and
  • The Church Choir rehearsal.

Members of the church are well commited to attending church programmes and these reflects greatly in their positive response to God's work. The Church is involved in social programs like caring and assistance to the needy people and also indigent students in tertiary institutions. The church could be described as a living church where living stones are made for christ and not dead stones.
